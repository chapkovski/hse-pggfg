  • In each round, you receive an endowment of {{ player.endowment }}. You decide how much of this endowment you want to "keep", and how much you want to "contribute". Each contribution x creates an amount of {{ Constants.coef_factor }}x for each group member (including the contributor). That means that for every ECU you contribute, the members of the whole group will be paid {{ Constants.coef_factor }} ECU each. For each ECU you contribute, you will be paid {{ Constants.coef_factor }} ECU, like all other group members. That part of your endowment that you do not contribute (i. e. that you “keep”), you keep for yourself.
  • Summing up in one formula, your earnings in ECU per round are as follows:
  • {{ player.endowment }} – Your Contribution + {{ Constants.coef_factor }} × (Sum of all group members’ contributions)
  • Please note that your contribution per round can be any amount between 0 and {{ player.endowment }} and that all group members are facing an identical decision situation. After each round you will be informed about the amount you contributed, the contributions of each of the other {{ Constants.num_others_per_group }} group members, your payoff based on the contributions of all group members, your payoff in the respective round and your payoff cumulated over all rounds. In addition, you will see a chart listing the average contribution of your group, and your contribution for all previous rounds.
